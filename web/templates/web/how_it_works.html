1

Tell Us What You Need

The client contacts us and shares their budget, preferred car type, model, year, and requirements. We search the Korean market, select several suitable options, send them to the client, discuss the details, and help choose the best match.

Budget AnalysisMarket SearchPersonalized Options
2

We Inspect the Car

Once a car is selected, our team visits the vehicle in person. We check diagnostics, body condition, mechanical state, interior, exterior, and overall quality. We record videos, take detailed photos, and send everything to the client with our honest recommendation.

200-Point CheckHD Video ReportHonest Assessment
3

We Continue or Reserve

If the car is not suitable, we continue searching and repeat the inspection process. If the car is in good condition and the client approves it, we reserve the vehicle.

No PressureUnlimited SearchClient Approval
4

Invoice & Payment

After reservation, we prepare an official invoice under the buyer's name and send it to the client. Once the payment is completed, we immediately purchase the car.

Official InvoiceSecure PaymentBuyer Protection
5

Export Preparation

We prepare all required documents, handle export procedures, and get the car ready for shipment. The vehicle is then moved to a secure storage yard while waiting for loading.

Full DocumentationExport HandlingSecure Storage
6

Shipping Report

When the car is ready for shipment, we load it for export. We record the loading process and send photo and video reports to the client, so they can follow the journey with confidence.

Loading ReportPhoto & Video ProofShipment Tracking
